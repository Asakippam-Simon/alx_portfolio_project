Welcome to eLearnHub

eLearnHub is my portfolio project intended to solve the problem of organizing and providing easy access to lecture materials, online quizzes, and mid-semester assessments for university lecturers. The project aims to create a centralized platform where lecturers can store and share their materials with students, as well as facilitate online assessments and quizzes.

About eLearnHub

eLearnHub is the end of foundation project as part of the ALX SE program where learners choose a project of their own to exhibit the skills they have aquired for the 8 months of learning.
